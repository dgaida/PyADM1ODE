# Mechanical Components

Mechanical components for material transport and mixing.

## Pump

Pumps are used to transfer substrate and digestate.

## Mixer

Mixers ensure homogenization and heat distribution in the digester.

## Troubleshooting

### Pump Not Delivering
- Check pressure head and blockages.

## Optimization Strategies
- Use intermittent mixing to save energy.

## Best Practices
- Size pumps for 80-90% of nominal flow.

## Sizing Guidelines
- Typical mixing power: 5-8 W/m³.
